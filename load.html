How To Load Customers

Our BJC Eload system allows resellers to send mobile load to different networks in the Philippines quickly using SMS commands.

Supported networks include Globe, Smart, TNT, DITO, and GOMO. Follow the guide below to send load to your customers.

SMS Loading Format

Example format:

LOAD <network> <number> <amount>

Example:

LOAD GLOBE 09123456789 50

After sending the command, the system will process your request and the customer will receive the load instantly.

Make sure you have enough balance in your reseller account before sending load to customers.

This system allows fast transactions, helping you serve customers quickly and grow your loading business.
